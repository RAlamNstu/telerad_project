Speech from marketing Director:

Teleradiology is the transmission of radiological patient images in multiple modalities from one location to another for the purposes of interpretation and sharing studies with other radiologists and physicians.
Teleradiology Solutions, (TRS) was founded in 2002 by two Yale University trained physicians. It provides teleradiology services (i.e. CT, MRI, X-ray, Ultrasound, Nuclear medicine, Echocardiograms) to over 150 hospitals in 20 countries globally including the United States, Singapore, Nigeria, Tanzania, Uganda, Maldives and remote parts of India. It is the first organization outside Singapore to be accredited by the Ministry of Health, Singapore and has provided teleradiology services to Singapore since 2005. In 2016 it won its first Indian government tender and has set up teleradiology services for the state of Tripura. Telerad Bangladesh Ltd introduced teleradiology service first time in Bangladesh with the own dedicated highest level server since 2013. We are able to offer final interpretation of imaging studies for all modalities (X-ray, CT Scan, MRI, USG, ECG and PET-CT .
Telerad Bangladesh Ltd delivers around-the-clock reading service with fast turnaround. Telerad Bangladesh Ltd is a web based imaging solution with proven expertise in medical informatics and we have expert & more experience radiologist in our Tele-radiology center who work are committed to give you better & faster Imaging solution. Telerad Bangladesh Ltd is successfully providing the teleradiology services about 500 prominent Diagnostic/Hospital and clinic all over the Bangladesh. including BRAC (TB Program all over the Bangladesh ) and JAICA (JAPAN Govt) Health screening program is providing our teleradiology service.
